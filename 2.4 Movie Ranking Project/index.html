Best Movie according to me

My top 3 movies of all time

Beetlejuice (2024)

Synopsis: Beetlejuice is back! After an unexpected family tragedy, three generations of the Deetz family return home to Winter River. Still haunted by the mischievous poltergeist Beetlejuice, they find themselves caught up in a madcap adventure filled with charming practical effects and ghoulish gags. Michael Keaton’s portrayal of Beetlejuice continues to delight audiences, making this a fun return to form for director Tim Burton1.

Speak No Evil (2024)

Synopsis: When an American family is invited to spend the weekend at the idyllic country estate of a charming British family, suspense ensues. James McAvoy’s captivating performance keeps viewers on the edge of their seats in this remake that manages to hush concerns of “been there, done that” by delivering unexpected twists and turns1

Rebel Ridge (2024)

Synopsis: Terry Richmond arrives in the town of Shelby Springs on a seemingly straightforward mission: post bail for his cousin and leave. However, things take a thrilling turn as he becomes embroiled in a gripping action-thriller. Aaron Pierre’s star-making performance elevates this film, which lays down the law on its contemporaries1
